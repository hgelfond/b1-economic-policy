By: Alex Armlovich


Mayor Bill de Blasio ran on a platform of ending the "Tale of Two Cities" in New York. Sympathizers and critics alike, however, note that local income inequality is not a direct operating variable of city government. The city's booming economy and status as a global "superstar city" make it all the harder to balance political interests in equity and growth.
