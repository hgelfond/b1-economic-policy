    A Data-Driven Look at Income Inequality
    from the Local, National, International and Private-Sector Perspectives


    Team Collaborators:
    Alex Armlovich, Hilary Gelfond, Jun Mukoyama, and Jake Schneider



    While income inequality has skyrocketed in recent years, federal debt has increased significantly, inhibiting economic growth and federal responses to a potential economic downturn.

    Meanwhile, asset values on equities are above their historical average since 2000, showing that stocks are expensive relative to their normal premium.

    All of this portends problems for the American economy.
